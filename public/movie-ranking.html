The Best Movies According to Rashid

My top 3 movies of all-time.

1. The Shawshank Redemption

It shows that hope is powerful, even in very hard times. Andy stays calm and strong no matter what happens to him. His friendship with Red is honest and meaningful. The ending feels rewarding and makes you feel good inside.

2. Home Alone

It’s fun and easy to watch. Kevin is smart and brave, even though he is just a kid. The traps are funny, and the movie feels warm and happy. It’s also great to watch with family, especially during the holidays.

3. Miss Peregrine’s Home for Peculiar Children

It has a magical and mysterious story. The characters are different and interesting, each with a special power. The movie feels like a dark fairy tale and takes you to a strange new world. It’s fun to watch and easy to get lost in.
